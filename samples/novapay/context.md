Strategic Partnership Assessment: Embedded Cross-Border Payments for Logistics SMEs
Background

NovaPay is a regional online payment provider serving small and medium-sized businesses across Central and Eastern Europe. The company is evaluating a partnership with GlobalFreight Connect, a logistics platform that serves over 120,000 importers and exporters across 42 countries.

The proposed partnership would integrate NovaPay's payment infrastructure directly into logistics workflows, allowing customers to manage freight payments, supplier settlements, customs fees, and invoices from a single platform.

The executive team has requested an assessment of the commercial opportunity, financial impact, risks, and expected return on investment.

Recommendation

The partnership presents a strong growth opportunity supported by favorable market trends, demonstrated customer demand, and attractive financial returns.

Under the base-case scenario, NovaPay is expected to generate €136M in cumulative additional revenue while achieving a projected ROI of 91%.

The project should proceed with a phased rollout beginning with pilot markets and clear adoption milestones.
